In recent years, the rapid growth of online education has transformed the way assessments are conducted, with remote examinations becoming increasingly common. 
However, ensuring fairness, security, and academic integrity in such exams remains a major challenge. 
Traditional manual invigilation methods are often resource-intensive, prone to human error, and difficult to scale for large numbers of students. 

Exam Shield is capable of detecting suspicious activities such as multiple faces in the frame, abnormal sounds, and unusual gaze movements.
This approach not only minimizes reliance on human proctors but also provides a secure, unbiased, and scalable solution for conducting online examinations effective.
